Frequently Asked
Questions

Can't find the answer you need? Feel free to contact our team directly. We are always happy to help!

Ask a Question

What should I expect during my first visit?

During your first visit, we will perform a thorough dental cleaning, take digital low-radiation X-rays, and conduct a detailed examination of your gums and teeth. Our dentist will then sit down with you to discuss a personalized treatment plan.

Do you accept dental insurance plans?

Yes, we accept most major PPO insurances, including Delta Dental, Aetna, Cigna, MetLife, and Blue Shield. Our billing team will verify your benefits and file all claims on your behalf to minimize out-of-pocket costs.

What financing options do you offer for large procedures?

For procedures not fully covered by insurance, we offer 0% interest monthly payment options directly through Rakshu Dental. We also partner with CareCredit to provide flexible long-term financing plans tailored to your budget.

How do I handle a dental emergency after hours?

If you experience a dental emergency after hours, call our office at +91 8870341570. Our voicemail system will route you directly to the on-call emergency dentist, or provide instruction on urgent pain management and care.

What comfort options do you have for anxious patients?

We offer nitrous oxide (laughing gas), mild oral sedation, and an extensive "Comfort Menu" featuring noise-canceling headphones, weighted blankets, herbal teas, and streaming services to make your visit completely stress-free.
